\section{Census}
\label{sec:census}

OMol-Descriptors-4M comprises 3{,}985{,}130 structures across 34 chemical
verticals, indexing 1{,}302{,}074 unique alphabetical formulas. At the
descriptor level the corpus carries 218{,}519{,}775 atom-level records,
30{,}203{,}025 charge-scheme records (per-job, per-method), 253{,}974{,}706
QTAIM bond critical points, and 1.58 billion bond-order records spanning
the three universal schemes. All counts in this section are corpus-level
totals prior to the train/val/test partition introduced in
Sec.~\ref{sec:eval-pipeline}.

\subsection{Bond records per vertical}
\label{sec:census-bonds}

Figure~\ref{fig:bond_scheme_breakdown} shows the per-vertical bond record
count, decomposed by scheme. Three bond-order schemes are reported on every
structure: Mayer (ORCA), L\"owdin (ORCA), and fuzzy bond order (Multiwfn).
At the corpus level L\"owdin contributes the largest share at
688{,}904{,}594 records (43.6\%), followed by fuzzy at 491{,}385{,}480
(31.1\%) and Mayer at 401{,}137{,}813 (25.4\%). Each row in the stacked
bar therefore corresponds to three independent estimates of the same
underlying bond, not three distinct bonds; cross-scheme agreement on those
shared bonds is treated separately in Sec.~\ref{sec:noise}.

The vertical-level distribution is heavy-tailed. The omol vertical alone
accounts for 680{,}873{,}135 records (43\% of the bond corpus); the two
\texttt{pdb\_fragments} verticals together contribute 159{,}038{,}457; the
DFT-relaxed \texttt{geom\_orca6} subset adds 76{,}494{,}584. The lower
end is set by \texttt{noble\_gas\_compounds} (1{,}404 records, 18
structures), which we retain as a stress case for sparse-bonding regimes.
Scheme mix is also non-uniform across verticals.
\texttt{electrolytes\_scaled\_sep} is Mayer-heavy (46\% of its bond
records), reflecting the polar bond profile of the scaled-separation
geometries; \texttt{rgd\_uks} is L\"owdin-dominant (44\%).

\subsection{Charge-scheme records per vertical}
\label{sec:census-charges}

Charge-scheme records track how many partial-charge methods executed
successfully on each structure. The corpus carries 30{,}203{,}025 records
across 3{,}985{,}130 structures, an average of 7.58 schemes per structure,
consistent with the six independent methods documented in
Sec.~\ref{sec:dataset} plus the ORCA-side Mulliken cross-check. Per-vertical
totals span four orders of magnitude, from \texttt{rmechdb}
(28{,}024 records over 2{,}803 structures) to omol (9{,}997{,}947
over 1{,}428{,}279). The full per-vertical breakdown is given in
Table~\ref{tab:t1_census} in the appendix.

Per-element-pair bond-order distributions and pairwise cross-method
agreement (charges, dipoles, bond orders) are reported in
Sec.~\ref{sec:noise}; the formula-hashed train/val/test
partition built from these counts is described in
Sec.~\ref{sec:eval}.
